A sphere-shaped primitive collider.

Variables

center

radius

See Also: BoxCollider, CapsuleCollider, PhysicMaterial, Rigidbody

Variables

SphereCollider.center

Vector3 center

The center of the sphere, measured in the object's local space.

// Reset the center to the transform's position
collider.center = Vector3.zero;

SphereCollider.radius

float radius

The radius of the sphere, measured in the object's local space. The sphere radius will be scaled by the transform's scale.

collider.radius = 10;
