How do I Make a Skybox?

Unity Manual > HOW-TOs > Graphics how-tos > How do I Make a Skybox?

A skybox is a 6-sided cube that is drawn behind all graphics in the camera. To create one, do as follows:

  1. Make 6 textures that correspond to each of the 6 sides of the skybox.
  2. Create a new material by choosing Assets->Create->Material from the main menu.
  3. Select the shader drop-down in the top of the inspector, choose RenderFX->Skybox from the drop-down.
  4. Assign the 6 textures to each texture slot in the material.

To Assign the skybox to the scene you're working on, do like this:

  1. Choose Edit->Render Settings from the main menu.
  2. Drag the material you created above to the Skybox Material slot in the inspector.
