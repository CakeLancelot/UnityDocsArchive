A Camera is a device through which the player views the world.

A screen space point is defined in pixels. The bottom-left of the screen is (0,0); the right-top is (pixelWidth,pixelHeight). The z position ranges from 0 to 1 (0 is on the near plane, 1 is on the far plane).

A viewport space point is normalized and relative to the camera. The bottom-left of the camera is (0,0); the top-right is (1,1). The z position ranges from 0 to 1 (0 is on the near plane, 1 is on the far plane).

A world space point is defined in global coordinates (eg. Transform.position).
