Lens Flare

Components > Rendering > Lens Flare

Lens flares simulate the effect of lights refracting inside a lens. They are used to represent really bright lights or, more subtly, just to add a bit more atmosphere to your scene.

Properties

Property:Function:
Flare Pointer to the Flare to render. The flare defines all aspects of the lens flare's appearance
Color Some flares can be colorized to better fit in with your scene's mood. if you have selected one of those flares, you can tint its color from here.
Brightness How large and bright the lens flare is

Details

You can attach lens flares to light components, or set them up separately. If you attach them to a light, they will autoconfigure. To get more precise control, use this component.

Hints

  • Be discrete about your usage of lens flares.
  • If you use a very bright lens flare, make sure it's direction fits with your scene's primary light source.
