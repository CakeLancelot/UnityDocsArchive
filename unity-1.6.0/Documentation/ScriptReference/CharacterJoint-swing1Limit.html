Usage

SoftJointLimit swing1Limit

Description

The limit around the primary axis of the character joint.

The limit is symmetric. Thus a value of eg. 30 will limit the rotation between -30 and 30. The limit is relative to the angle the two rigidbodies started the simulation out with.
