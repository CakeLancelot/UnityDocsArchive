Quaternion.operator *

Quaternion operator * (Quaternion lhs, Quaternion rhs)

Concatenates rotations lhs and rhs. Rotating a point first with lhs, and then with rhs is the same as rotating the point by the concatenated rotation. Please note that rotation's do not commute, which means: "lhs * rhs" does not equal "rhs * lhs"

Quaternion.Lerp

Interpolates from towards to by t and normalizes the result afterwards. This is faster than Slerp but looks worse if the rotations are far apart.
