ParticleRenderer.lengthScale

float lengthScale

How much are the particle stretched in their direction of motion. Use this to make particles always be longer than they are wide.

ParticleRenderer.velocityScale

float velocityScale

How much are the particles strectched depending on ''how fast they move'' Use this to make particles get longer as their speed increases.

ParticleRenderer.cameraVelocityScale

float cameraVelocityScale

How much are the particles strected depending on the Camera's speed Use this to make particles become large if the viewing camera has a large speed.

ParticleRenderer.maxPartileSize

float maxPartileSize

Clamp the mazimum particle size. Particles can become very heavy on fillrate. Use this setting to make sure they don't use too much perfomance when up close to the viewer.
