Optimizing iPhone Performance

Making games for the iPhone is fun. But making your game run smoothly can affect its success. Therefore, it is important to understand the graphical capabilities and processing power of the iPhone.

You should expect the iPhone's capabilities to be somewhere between the Nintendo DS and the PlayStation Portable. Please, plan your games accordingly!

Thankfully, Unity is there for you! We have spent a lot of time and energy making Unity-created iPhone run fast. Below we have gathered some guidelines which will help you to achieve better performance on the iPhone.
