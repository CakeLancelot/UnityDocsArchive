Class Variables

Texture.masterTextureLimit

int masterTextureLimit

Limits the texture quality of all textures. High texture limits save graphics memory and make old graphics cards run your game faster at the expense of visual quality. A texture limit of 0 uploads textures at the highest resolution to the graphics card. A texture limit of 1 uploads textures half the original size, 2 uploads textures at a quarter of the original size.

Texture.anisotropicFiltering

AnisotropicFiltering anisotropicFiltering

Anisotropic filtering looks better on textures when viewed at an angle, but comes at a cost in the graphics hardware. 'AnisotropicFiltering.Enable' uses the anisotropic filtering value specified in the texture. (Default) 'AnisotropicFiltering.Disable' disables anisotropic filtering on all textures. 'AnisotropicFiltering.ForceEnable' enables anisotropic filtering on all textures.
