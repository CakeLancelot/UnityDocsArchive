Option for how to apply a force using Rigidbody.AddForce.

Values
Force

Add a force to the rigid body.

VelocityChange

Makes the object change it's velocity by the given amount.

Acceleration

Adds a force to the rigid body factoring out the mass of the body.

Impulse

Apply the impulse to the rigid body.
