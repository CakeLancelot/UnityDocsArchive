The center of the box.

Vector3 size

The size of the box. size.x is the width of the box size.y is the height of the box size.z is the depth of the box

Bounds.extents

Vector3 extents

The extents of the box. This is size / 2

Bounds.min

Vector3 min

The minimal point of the box. min = center - extents;

Bounds.max

Vector3 max

The maximal point of the box. min = center + extents;
