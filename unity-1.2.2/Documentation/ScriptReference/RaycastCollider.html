Collider

A line based collider.

Variables

center

length

Raycast colliders are mainly used to model cars because they offer more accurate collision detection. A Raycast collider always casts one ray downwards the local z-axis. Raycast colliders are most useful when used in combination with physic materials that are setup to be springy. See Also: BoxCollider, SphereCollider, DynamicsMaterial, RigidBody

Variables

RaycastCollider.center

Vector3 center

The center of the capsule, measured in the object's local space.

// Reset the center to the transform's position
collider.center = Vector3.zero;

RaycastCollider.length

float length

The length of the ray cast measured in local space. The length will be scaled by the transform's scale.

collider.height = 2;
